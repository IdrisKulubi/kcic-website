# KCIC Website User Manual

Welcome to the Kenya Climate Innovation Centre (KCIC) Website Administration Manual. This document provides step-by-step instructions on how to manage the website's content and features via the Admin Panel.

---

## 1. Accessing the Admin Panel

To make changes to the website, you must log in to the secure Admin Panel.

*   **URL:** `[Website Domain]/admin` (e.g., `https://kenyacic.org/admin`)
*   **Credentials:** Enter your assigned admin email and password.

### The Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will be greeted by the **Admin Dashboard**. The sidebar on the left provides access to all content modules:
*   **Hero Section**: Manage the homepage sliders.
*   **Stats**: Update impact numbers.
*   **Programs**: Manage programme details.
*   **Opportunities**: Post jobs, tenders, and RFPs.
*   **News**: Publish articles and press releases.
*   **Team**: Manage staff and board profiles.
*   **Partners**: Update partner logos.
*   **Whistleblower**: View confidential reports.

---

## 2. Content Management Modules

### A. Hero Section
The Hero Section is the large slider at the top of the homepage.
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Hero Section` in the sidebar.
*   **Add New:** Click "Add Slide". Upload a high-quality image, add a title, subtitle, and an optional "Learn More" link.
*   **Edit/Delete:** Use the "Edit" (pencil) or "Delete" (trash) icons next to an existing slide.
*   **Reorder:** Drag and drop slides to change their display order.

### B. Impact Statistics
These numbers appear on the homepage to showcase KCIC's achievements.
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Stats`.
*   **Update:** Click on a statistic (e.g., "Enterprises Incubated") to update the numerical value or label.

### C. Programmes
Manage the "Our Programmes" pages (e.g., GreenBiz, Agribiz).
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Programs`.
*   **Add New:** Click "Add Programme".
    *   **Title & Summary:** Essential for the listing page.
    *   **Description:** The main content of the detailed page.
    *   **Cover Image:** Displayed on the listing card.
*   **Edit:** Select a programme to update its text or images.

### D. Opportunities (Jobs & Procurement)
This module handles all external listings: **Careers** (Jobs/Internships) and **Procurement** (Tenders/RFPs/Consultancies).
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Opportunities`.
*   **Add New:** Click "Add Opportunity".
    *   **Type:** Select "Job", "Consultancy", "RFP", or "Tender". This automatically routes the post to the correct page (Careers vs. Procurement).
    *   **Attachments:** You can upload PDF documents (e.g., full TORs) directly here.
    *   **Status:** "Active" posts are visible. Toggle to "Closed" to hide them without deleting.

### E. Newsroom
Manage the blog and news updates.
*   **Navigation:** Go to `News`.
*   **Add New:** Click "Create Article".
    *   **Category:** Choose a category (e.g., "Press Release", "Event", "Success Story").
    *   **Content:** Use the rich text editor to format your article.
    *   **Publish Date:** Articles are sorted by this date.

### F. Our Team
Manage the profiles displayed on the "Staff" and "Board" pages.
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Team`.
*   **Add Member:** Click "Add Member".
    *   **category:** Select "Staff", "Management", or "Board".
    *   **Bio:** A brief professional biography.
    *   **Social Links:** Optional links to LinkedIn or Twitter.

### G. Partners
Manage the logos displayed in the "Partners" scroll.
*   **Navigation:** Go to `Partners`.
*   **Add Partner:** Upload a logo and provide the partner's name.

---

## 3. Operational Features

### Whistleblower Reports
The website includes a confidential reporting tool for "Whistleblower" submissions.
*   **Access:** Go to `Whistleblower` in the sidebar. **Note:** Access to this section may be restricted to specific high-level admins.
*   **Viewing:** Click on a report ID to view the details description and category.
*   **Status:** You can update the status (e.g., "New", "Investigating", "Resolved", "Dismissed") to track internal processing.
*   **Security:** These reports are stored securely and should be treated with high confidentiality.

## 4. Best Practices

### Recommended Image Dimensions
To ensure the website looks professional, please stick to these recommended image ratios:

| Section | Recommended Size (px) | Format |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hero Slides** | 1920 x 1080 | JPG / WebP |
| **Programme Covers** | 800 x 600 (4:3) | JPG / WebP |
| **Team Photos** | 400 x 400 (Square) | JPG |
| **Partner Logos** | 300px width (limit height) | PNG (Transparent) |
| **News Thumbnails** | 800 x 450 (16:9) | JPG |

### SEO Tips
*   **Titles:** Keep titles clear and descriptive.
*   **Descriptions:** Write unique summaries for programmes and news articles.
*   **Images:** Compress images before uploading to ensure fast page load speeds.
